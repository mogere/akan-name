Your Akan name

In Ghana, according to Akan culture, once a child is born, their first name they are given depends entirely on the day of the week they have been born. For every day of the week, there is a special specific name which you are given; of course depending on your gender also ;). In this application, we will take you on a trip to Ghana to find out what your name would be. If you do not know which day you were born, worry not, just fill in the details as requested below and the app will do it all for you.
